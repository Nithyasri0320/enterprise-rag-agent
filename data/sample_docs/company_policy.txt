EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK — LEAVE AND ATTENDANCE POLICY

Paid Leave
Full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid leave per year, accrued
monthly at a rate of 1.5 days per month. Unused leave up to 10 days may be
carried over into the following calendar year. Leave requests must be
submitted through the HR portal at least 5 working days in advance for
planned leave.

Sick Leave
Employees receive 12 days of paid sick leave per year, separate from the
paid leave allowance above. A medical certificate is required for sick
leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

Work From Home Policy
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
Engineering teams on active on-call rotations must be available on video
call within 15 minutes during business hours regardless of location.

Maternity and Paternity Leave
Maternity leave is 26 weeks as per statutory requirements. Paternity leave
is 10 working days, to be taken within 6 months of the child's birth.

Termination and Notice Period
The standard notice period for resignation is 60 days for employees at the
senior engineer level and above, and 30 days for all other full-time roles.
During probation (first 90 days of employment), the notice period is 7 days.

Reimbursement Policy
Business travel expenses must be submitted within 15 days of the expense
being incurred, with original receipts attached. Internet and phone
reimbursement for remote employees is capped at ₹1,500 per month.
