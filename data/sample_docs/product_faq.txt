PRODUCT FAQ — CLOUDDESK SUBSCRIPTION PLATFORM

Refunds and Cancellations
Customers can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase, no questions
asked. After 30 days, refunds are prorated based on unused subscription
months, minus a 10% processing fee. To request a refund, contact
support@clouddesk.example with your order ID.

Subscription Tiers
CloudDesk offers three tiers: Starter (up to 5 users, $12/user/month),
Growth (up to 50 users, $19/user/month, includes SSO and audit logs), and
Enterprise (unlimited users, custom pricing, includes dedicated support and
a 99.95% uptime SLA).

Data Export
All plans allow full data export in CSV or JSON format at any time from
Settings > Data Export. Exported data includes all user records, activity
logs from the last 12 months, and configuration settings. Enterprise plan
customers can additionally request a full database dump via support.

API Rate Limits
The REST API is rate-limited to 100 requests per minute on the Starter
tier, 500 requests per minute on Growth, and 2,000 requests per minute on
Enterprise. Rate limit headers are included in every API response.

Security and Compliance
CloudDesk is SOC 2 Type II certified. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256)
and in transit (TLS 1.3). Enterprise customers can request a signed DPA
(Data Processing Agreement) and access to third-party penetration test
summaries under NDA.

Downtime and Support SLA
Enterprise customers with an active SLA receive a 15-minute response time
guarantee for Severity 1 incidents (complete service outage), and service
credits equal to 10% of the monthly fee for every hour of downtime beyond
the 99.95% uptime commitment.
